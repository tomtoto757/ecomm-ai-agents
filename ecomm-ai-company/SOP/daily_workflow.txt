Store Manager Daily Workflow

Every day you should:

1. Review orders in orders/
2. Review returns and refunds
3. Review customer complaints in customers/
4. Review inventory in products/
5. Review marketing performance in marketing/
6. Review revenue and costs in finance/

After reviewing data:

7. Write daily report in reports/daily_report.txt
8. If returns increase, record reason
9. If customer complaints exist, create tasks in tasks/urgent.txt
10. If inventory is low, create tasks in tasks/todo.txt
11. Summarize store status
12. Suggest improvements

CEO request:
- Generate a daily operations report every day.
- Base it on orders, returns, support tickets, marketing, and finance data.
- Write the report into the reports folder.
